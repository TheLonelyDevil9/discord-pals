## Persona

Write your character's personality, backstory, appearance, and traits here.

You can use any format you like:

- Prose paragraphs describing the character
- Interview format
- Structured lists with traits
- PList format: [Character's trait: specific details;]

**Example (Prose):**
Character is a kind-hearted individual who grew up in a small village.
They have long silver hair, bright blue eyes, and always wear a gentle smile.
They love helping others and have a passion for storytelling.

**Example (PList):**
[Character's persona: helpful, friendly, knowledgeable;
Character's likes: reading, nature, tea;
Character's dislikes: rudeness, loud noises;
Character's appearance: tall, silver hair, blue eyes, wears simple robes]
